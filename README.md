# Manufacturing-Operations-Dashboard---Falcon-Manufacturing-LLC
An end-to-end, multi-page Power BI dashboard engineered to monitor, analyze, and optimize manufacturing operations. This business intelligence solution provides deep insights across production performance, quality control, financial overheads, and inventory management—enabling data-driven executive decision-making.
# Manufacturing Operations Dashboard - Falcon Manufacturing LLC
## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The report consists of 4 specialized analysis modules:
1. **Executive Overview:** High-level operational and financial performance overview.
2. **Production Analytics:** Efficiency metrics, targets tracking, and shift performance.
3. **Quality Analytics:** Deep-dive into defect rates, scrap overheads, and city-wise defect distributions.
4. **Inventory & Cost:** Supply chain valuations, material tracking, and warehouse distributions.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Operational Insights

### 1. Executive & Production Performance
*   **Production Volume:** Tracks Total Production (**53M units**) against a target of **59M units**, with real-time target variance monitoring (-7M).
*   **OEE & Efficiency:** Real-time visibility into overall equipment effectiveness and operational efficiency standing at **88.92%**.
*   **Geographic & Category Breakdown:** Evaluates production across key regions (UAE, Saudi Arabia, India, Oman) and sectors (Industrial Machines, Steel Components, Automotive, Construction).

### 2. Financial Overview
*   **Revenue & Cost Tracking:** Monitors **AED 197bn** in Total Revenue against **AED 125bn** in Total Cost, yielding a Gross Profit of **AED 72bn** (a +103.87% growth YoY).
*   **Cost Drivers:** Granular breakdowns of operational costs by department (Production, Maintenance, Quality Assurance) and primary supplier allocations.

### 3. Quality Assurance & Analytics
*   **Defect Metrics:** Tracks Defective Units (**2M**) alongside a calculated **3.95% Defect Rate**.
*   **Scrap & Rework Financials:** Quantifies the financial impact of errors, capturing **AED 1.6bn** in Scrap Costs and **AED 1.1bn** in Rework Costs.
*   **Quality Scores:** Measures a holistic baseline Quality Score (**85**) alongside monthly quality trend graphs.

### 4. Supply Chain & Inventory
*   **Material Valuations:** Monitors **AED 6.6bn** in current Inventory Value alongside **AED 69.5bn** in raw material costs.
*   **Logistics Footprint:** Visualizes inventory value distribution across primary storage infrastructure (Beta Logistics Center, Gulf Storage Hub, Eastern Depot, Alpha Warehouse).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Advanced Skills Used

*   **BI Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
*   **Data Architecture:** Star Schema implementation utilizing highly optimized Fact and Dimension tables (including custom Date dimensions).
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Engineered advanced calculations for Time Intelligence (YoY Growth %, Prior Year [PY] benchmarks), moving averages, target variances, and financial aggregations.
*   **UI/UX Design:** Professional color-blocked, multi-page navigation system built with a responsive sidebar template designed for quick scanning and structural clarity.
